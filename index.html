My Favorite Hobby: Playing Madden

Why I Love Playing Madden

Playing Madden allows me to enjoy football in a virtual environment. It's fun to compete and improve my skills with each game.

I got into playing Madden after my friends and I played together during high school. Since then, I've been enjoying learning new ways to play the game, as well as learning about the players so that I can follow football more closely.

  • Materials needed: Gaming console, controller, and a copy of the game
  • Benefits: Competitive fun, strategy development, team management skills
  • Favorite aspect: Spending time with friends and competing for rewards
  1. Select the game mode (Franchise, Ultimate Team, or Exhibition).
  2. Choose your team or create a custom roster.
  3. Play the game and score to win.
